EARTHQUAKE


  • Keep a radio set handy for instructions, warnings and helplines.
  • Pack important items like eatables, water bottles, dust masks and flashlights.
  • Immediately turn off gas and electicity when shaking begins.
  • Take cover under a table or a doorframe.
  • Stay away from furniture that can fall on you.
  • If in bed, protect your head with a pillow.
  • If outdoors, find an open space away from buildings, trees and power lines.
  • If in a car, slow down and drive to an open space.
