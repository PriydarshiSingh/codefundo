FLOOD


  • Keep a radio set handy for instructions, warnings and helplines.
  • Pack important items like eatables and medicines.
  • In case of evacuation warnings, move to higher grounds.
  • Turn of all switches and disconnect all electrical appliances.
  • Avoid walking through moving water.
  • Do not drive in areas where floodwaters have receded. The weakened roads could collapse.
  • Stay away from fallen power lines.
  • Do not enter buildings unless authorities advise you to.
