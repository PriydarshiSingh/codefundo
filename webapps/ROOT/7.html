WILDFIRE


  • Contact the fire department as soon as possible.
  • Avoid spilling flammable liquids when camping.
  • Do not discard matches and cigarettes anywhere on the ground.
  • Plan an evacuation route.
  • Wear protective clothing and footwear.
  • Wear masks to protect against flying ashes.
  • Look for a water body to crouch in.
  • Find a depressed area with little or no vegetation.
  • Lie low to the ground and stay covered.
  • Inhale air through moist cloth.
